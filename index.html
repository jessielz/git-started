Mikasa Ackerman

Highly regarded by the Survey Corps as a person who is easily worth a hundred soldiers, Mikasa Ackerman graduated at the top of her cadet class in the 104th Training Corps. She is the adopted sister of the protagonist Eren Yeager (alternatively spelled as Jaeger) and the last known person of Asian descent in the universe of Shingeki no Kyojin (Attack on Titan).
